A lighter with realistic flame effects.

Hold the left mouse button to light the lighter.

Load "Lighter_with_Flame_FX.unity" for an example scene, or simply drag the Lighter.prefab from the Prefabs folder (in the Project window) into the Hierarchy window.

Have fun!
